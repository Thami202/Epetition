Petition

Addressing the issue of Load shedding

The Government needs to address the issue of load shedding as it is affecting the lives of citizens and business are losing alot of profits , The country economy is also going down due to this problem , which means more money needs to be invested in eletricity in order to end load shedding.

More details

It has been reported that the lack of electricity has affected hospitals and other critical infrastructure, causing delays in medical treatment and equipment failures. Furthermore, it can also have a negative impact on businesses, causing losses in productivity and revenue. Additionally, load shedding can also cause stress and anxiety for individuals, especially those who rely on electricity for basic needs such as cooking and lighting.
