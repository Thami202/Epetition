Petition

Addressing the issues of high tuition fees in our university.

There are several ways that the government can address the issue of high tuition fees, Increase funding for higher education: The government can allocate more funds to universities and colleges to help keep tuition fees low.

More details

The government can allocate more funds to universities and colleges to help keep tuition fees low , The government can establish programs that allow students to borrow money for tuition, with the understanding that they will pay back the loans based on their income after graduation , he government can implement policies that make certain levels of education, such as undergraduate degrees, free for all students.

Parliament decided not to debate this petition

Because it has not been possible to debate this petition to date and the request made by this petition has been considered by the Committee and in the House of Commons in other ways, the Committee has agreed not schedule this petition for debate.

Government responded

This response was given on 03 January 2023

As the Government we are concerns about the cost of providing free education, concerns about the quality of education that would be provided if it were free, and concerns about the impact that free education would have on the economy. Additionally, we believe that individuals should be responsible for paying for their own education, as it can be seen as a personal investment in one's own future.
